## 1 --- Absolute Basics (everyone learns these first)

- Ollie

- Pop shove-it (BS shove)

- Frontside 180

- Backside 180

- Frontside shove-it (FS shove)

## 2 --- First Flip Tricks (entry-level flips)

- Kickflip

- Heelflip

*(These two unlock almost every flip variation later.)*

## 3 --- Early Variations (moderately hard but very learnable)

- Varial kickflip

- Varial heelflip

- FS flip (FS 180 kickflip)

- BS flip (BS 180 kickflip)

- FS heel

- BS heel

## 4 --- Shove/Spin Progressions (building board control)

- 360 pop shove-it

- 360 frontside shove-it

- Bigspin

- FS bigspin

## 5 --- Intermediate--Advanced Core Tricks

*These are the "real street skater" benchmarks.*

- Tre flip (360 kickflip)

- Inward heelflip

- Hardflip

## 6 --- Advanced Variations

- Bigspin flip (bigflip)

- Bigspin heel

- Dolphin flip

- Impossible

## 7 --- Pressure / Caspers (tech category)

(Usually learned after solid flip control.)

- Hospital flip

- Casper flip

- Anti-flip / Plasma flip

## 8 --- Expert-Level / Rare Tricks

- Double kickflip

- Double heelflip

- Triple kickflip

- 720 shove-it

- Laser flip

- 360 heelflip

- Dragon flip

- Merlin twist
