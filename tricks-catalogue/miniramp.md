# **1 --- Foundations (must learn to even "skate" the miniramp)**

These establish balance, pumping, and coping control.

- Pumping to gain speed

- Rock to fakie

- Kickturn (frontside)

- Kickturn (backside)

- Rock 'n' roll (BS)

- Rock 'n' roll (FS)

- Axle stall (BS)

- Axle stall (FS)

# **2 --- Entry-Level Real Tricks**

Once you can stall consistently.

- BS axle stall to fakie

- FS axle stall to fakie

- 50-50 grind (BS)

- 50-50 grind (FS)

- BS disaster

- FS disaster

- Tail stall

- Nose stall

- Early grab rock to fakie (fun/confidence trick)

# **3 --- Learning to Use the Coping**

These build commitment and board control.

- BS 5-0 stall

- FS 5-0 stall

- BS feeble stall

- FS feeble stall

- BS smith stall

- FS smith stall

- BS blunt to fakie

- FS blunt to fakie

*(FS blunt often feels easier than BS blunt for many skaters.)*

# **4 --- Basic Lip Tricks & Reverts**

Now you start sliding or pivoting.

- BS slash grind

- FS slash grind

- BS pivot

- FS pivot

- BS pivot to fakie

- FS pivot to fakie

- BS revert on the wall

- FS revert on the wall

# **5 --- Nose & Tail Work**

Adding finesse.

- BS nose stall to fakie

- FS nose stall to fakie

- BS tail stall to fakie

- FS tail stall to fakie

- Nose pivot (BS)

- Nose pivot (FS)

# **6 --- Advanced Lip / Coping Tricks**

Where style and control start becoming real.

- BS 5-0 grind

- FS 5-0 grind

- BS smith grind

- FS smith grind

- BS feeble grind

- FS feeble grind

- BS tail stall revert

- FS tail stall revert

- BS blunt to rock

- FS blunt to rock

# **7 --- Tech Miniramp Moves (serious skill starts here)**

These require precision and consistency.

- Nosepick (BS)

- Nosepick (FS)

- Nose pivot to revert

- BS axle to BS disaster

- FS axle to FS disaster

- Blunt pivot

- Backtail stall

- Frontside tail stall

- Backtail revert

- Frontside tail revert

# **8 --- Flip Tricks IN or OUT (Advanced / Pro Level)**

Miniramp flips are uncommon and difficult.

- Rock to fakie **kickflip out\**

- Axle stall **kickflip out\**

- Blunt to **kickflip out\**

- Kickflip rock to fakie

- Kickflip rock 'n' roll

- Heelflip rock to fakie

- Fingerflip rock to fakie

- Blunt fingerflip out

# **9 --- Elite / Endgame Miniramp Tricks**

Very few skaters do these with consistency.

- Kickflip back disaster

- Kickflip front disaster

- Kickflip blunt

- Heelflip blunt

- Lien to tail (in mini!)

- Smith → blunt → revert

- Blunt to fakie **flip out\**

- Bigspin disaster

- Bigspin back disaster

- Bigspin front disaster

- Tre flip rock to fakie

- Hardflip rock to fakie

- Hardflip disaster
